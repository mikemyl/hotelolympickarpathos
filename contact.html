About us

Olympic Hotel in Pigadia, Karpathos is located near the main beach (only 500 metres away). It stands on a small hillside, overlooking the Aegean sea and the main town of Pigadia. All services and sights are within easy reach. A wonderful variety of tourist shops, gourmet restaurants and tavernas, cafes and bars are easily accessible, while the post office, travel agencies and all other amenities are also nearby.

The ancient temple of Poseidon, a main tourist attraction, is located near the main road, which runs parallel to the coastline, and is within walking distance. The central bus stop, located only a few hundred metres away from the hotel, runs regular bus services to and from villages and beaches. This is a scenic and inexpensive way to enjoy some of Karpathos’s main attractions. Renting a car or motorbike can be arranged by Hotel reception or by visiting the nearby rent-a-car facilities.

Hotel Olympic offers cozy and comfortable common rooms, including a lounge with TV, books, a dining room for breakfast and lunch (both indoor and outdoor, by the swimming pool) and a stylish bar. There is free internet service (computers are available in the reception area) and free Wi-Fi access in all areas and rooms of the hotel. Outdoor facilities include a swimming pool, a baby’s pool, a children’s playground, a ping-pong table and a beautiful garden.

Olympic Hotel offers 3 studios, 3 single rooms and 36 double rooms, fully equipped with air-conditioning, TV, radio, telephone, refrigerator and hairdryer. All rooms are especially spacious, comfortable and quiet, with a sizeable verandah. There are sea-view, mountain-view and garden view rooms available in the hotel.

Olympic Hotel also offers spacious private parking facilities for your convenience.

The staff of Olympic Hotel wishes to make your holidays particularly memorable. You will feel right at home in our warm and hospitable environment. The hotel manager, receptionists and catering staff are available 24 hours a day and are always willing to attend to your every need. Our main concern is that customers are more than satisfied by our services and leave our hotel feeling relaxed and happy. We are always delighted to see more and more of our customers coming back each year and keeping in touch over the winter as well.
